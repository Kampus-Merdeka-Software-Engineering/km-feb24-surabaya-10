Insight

Statistic of Dwellings sales and overall class category sales show relatively similar lines. The possible sale of Dwellings has impacted property sales in New York City.

The best-selling Dwellings property sales data was in Queens (4) and The lowest total sales are in Borough 1 (Manhattan).

The Dwellings category with the One Family Dwellings type is more popular with customers than other categories.

The least popular category is the Three Family Dwellings category.
